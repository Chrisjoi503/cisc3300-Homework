2. What is normalization in SQL? 
Normalization organizes the columns and tables of a database to eliminate redundancy and inconsistent dependencies. It makes sure that 
database flexible and protected,

3. What are primary and foreign keys? 
Primary Keys is what we use to identify a row and foreign keys are the primary key of a row in a different table 

4. What are the three relationship types? 
The three relationship types are one-to-one, one-to-many, and many-to-many. 

5. What is the difference between a left join and an inner join?
 A left join will provide you all records of the left table and the macthing records on the right table and a inner join will select records 
 that have matching values in both tables.
